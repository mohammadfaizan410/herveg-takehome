Welcome to HERVeg.05 - Empowering Women Farmers in Tanzania

At HERVeg.05, we believe in the transformative power of agriculture to improve lives. Our mission is to empower women farmers in Tanzania by providing them with the tools, knowledge, and resources to grow and sell high-quality vegetables. We connect farmers to valuable information, training, and market opportunities.

About Us

HERVeg.05 is a social enterprise dedicated to reducing malnutrition rates within Tanzanian farming communities, with a specific focus on empowering women and children. Our HerConnect app serves as a platform to electronically register farmers, provide access to essential information and training, and offer market opportunities.
